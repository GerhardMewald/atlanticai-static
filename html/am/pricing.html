A different commercial logic

Atlantic AI is built for hotels that want to own the booking relationship, not rent it from intermediaries.

We provide the infrastructure required to convert more direct demand: conversion-oriented hotel website, IBE, PSP adapter layer, hosting, tracking, data ownership, and AI/ML readiness.

The pricing follows the same philosophy: light fixed infrastructure pricing, plus a small success-linked participation where measurable value is created.
