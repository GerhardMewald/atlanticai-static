You are subscribing to Atlantic AI IBE Infrastructure at €5 per room per month plus 2% of direct-booking turnover processed through the Atlantic AI direct-booking channel.
Today (initial charge): You are charged a pro-rated portion of the monthly infrastructure fee based on the time remaining in the current billing period. The exact amount is calculated by Stripe at checkout.

Recurring billing: Starting from the next billing cycle, your payment method will be charged automatically on a monthly basis.

Monthly invoice structure: Each monthly invoice includes:
• the upcoming month’s infrastructure subscription (€5 per room), and
• the retrospectively calculated 2% participation on direct-booking turnover generated in the previous month.

Room count basis: The billable room count is validated against your Apaleo inventory and may be adjusted accordingly.

VAT treatment: Determined based on your billing country and company details and applied on the official invoice.
